Meet your Neighbears


Turn strangers next door into a friendly community. Share, connect & enjoy everyday neighborhood life.

In a world where we can stay connected with someone we met 10 years ago on the other side of the globe, we often don’t even know our next-door neighbor. Neighbears changes that. Our app helps you connect with the people in your own building – with names, photos, and optional details like floor or door location.

Why Neighbears?
  • 🤝 Easy contact with your neighbors
  • 🌱 Everyday help (plants, pets, sugar, eggs)
  • 🎉 More community & understanding
  • 📬 Create events with just an emoji – from “extra carrots” 🥕 to “anyone up for table tennis?” 🏓

How it works:
  • 📩 Sign up with your address
  • 🏠 See who lives in your building
  • 🧡 Share, connect & enjoy your friendly neighborhood
